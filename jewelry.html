Fashion for Change Winter 2023 Showcase: Joy Design Scene Catwalk.

Models: Wendy Ly, Ashleen Sandhu, Mehreen, Diya Shah.

Taken by: Joyce Tan


At the end of each winter term, Fashion for Change hosts a showcase where creatives get to collaborate. Artists, models, designers, photographers, videographers, and more are welcome. Last year's theme explored a variety of emotions that a soul goes through. I had the honour of creating the design scene for Joy. This experience was one of a kind for me. I worked on the editing of the song "Sweet Life" by Frank Ocean, the choreography, outfit styling, lighting coordination, and of course the jewelry design.


Through this scene, I explored the joy of friends getting together, something that gives us a "sweet life." Four friends travel to a beach, from sunrise to sunset. For this scene, I made four wire wrapped crystal jewelry pieces. These four pieces represent both the colours and energy of joy. They are red onyx, citrine, mookaite jasper, pearls, tiger's eye, and yellow moonstone.
